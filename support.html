Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How does Homezai's AI scheduling work?
Homezai uses artificial intelligence to automatically coordinate showing times based on buyer availability, agent calendars, and property access windows. The system learns from your scheduling patterns to suggest optimal times.
Can I use my own branding with Homezai?
Yes! Homezai offers full white-label capabilities. You can customize the platform with your association, MLS, brokerage, team, or agent branding including logos, colors, and domain.
Which MLS and CRM systems does Homezai integrate with?
Homezai integrates with multiple MLS systems and popular CRMs used in the real estate industry. Contact our team for a full list of supported integrations for your market.
How do I optimize routes for multiple property showings?
Our automated multi-home routing feature uses intelligent algorithms to plan the most efficient route across multiple properties, factoring in drive times, showing durations, and scheduling constraints.
Who owns the data in Homezai?
You do. Homezai maintains a strict data ownership policy. All client data, property information, and showing records belong entirely to you. You can export your data at any time.
How much does Homezai cost?
Homezai offers flexible pricing plans based on the size of your organization and feature requirements. Contact our sales team for a customized quote.
Can I try Homezai before purchasing?
Yes! We offer personalized demos where you can see the platform in action. Schedule a demo through our website to get started.
What kind of support does Homezai provide?
We provide comprehensive support including training videos, documentation, email support, phone support (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM EST), and dedicated account managers for enterprise clients.
How long does it take to set up Homezai?
Most organizations can be fully set up and running within a few days. Our onboarding team guides you through the process, including MLS integration and team setup.
Is my data secure with Homezai?
Absolutely. Homezai uses industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. We are compliant with all relevant data protection regulations.
